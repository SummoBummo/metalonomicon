Our Mission

Our mission is to create a dedicated platform for metal fans to stay informed and connected by providing up-to-date, comprehensive coverage of bands, events, and the vibrant culture that surrounds them. We strive to deliver accurate and engaging content, from band profiles and album releases to concert announcements and festival reviews, ensuring fans never miss a beat. By fostering a community of passionate metalheads, we aim to be the go-to source for discovering new music, supporting live events, and celebrating the relentless energy of the metal scene.

Who We Are

The team behind the metal site is a passionate group of metalheads with a wealth of experience in both the music industry and digital media. Comprised of writers, editors, event coordinators, and moderators, they work collaboratively to ensure the site provides accurate and timely information on the latest band updates, album releases, and upcoming events. Each team member brings their own unique expertise, whether it's in covering live concerts, conducting interviews with bands, or keeping track of local and international festivals. Their deep-rooted knowledge of the metal scene fuels their commitment to supporting the global metal community, delivering content that both informs and engages fans. Through their collective efforts, the team strives to create a platform that not only keeps fans up-to-date but also celebrates the culture and diversity of the metal genre.

Meet Us

Summer Hall - Founder

Driven by her deep love for metal music, she decided to channel their passion into creating a website that celebrates the genre in all its forms. From album reviews to live show recaps, her site serves as a hub for fellow fans to connect, discover new bands, and explore metal’s rich history. With a sharp eye for design and a vast knowledge of the scene, she curates content that resonates with both long-time metalheads and newcomers alike.

Katya Grimstorm - Author/Editor

Fueled by her deep admiration for metal, she write articles that explore the intricacies of the genre, from in-depth album reviews to interviews with rising bands. With a keen ear for detail and a passion for storytelling, her pieces offer insights that engage both die-hard fans and those new to the scene. Through her writing, she aims to share the raw energy and emotion of metal music, while fostering a deeper understanding of its diverse subcultures.

Axel Graham - Moderator

He’s a dedicated metal fan with a no-nonsense attitude when it comes to moderating the online community, ensuring that every discussion stays on track and true to the genre's spirit. With a keen sense of order, he enforces strict guidelines, swiftly removing any off-topic or disrespectful content to maintain the site’s integrity. Despite his firm approach, his deep knowledge of metal and passion for the music make him a respected figure in this community.

Zara Steel - Moderator

She’s a passionate metalhead who channels her love for the genre into moderating an online community where fellow fans can discuss, share, and discover new music. With a sharp eye for detail and a deep knowledge of metal, she ensures the site stays welcoming and focused on the music while keeping the discussions respectful and engaging.
