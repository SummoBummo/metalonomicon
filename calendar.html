Selected Event

Metallica

March 14,2025 | 7:00pm | The Filmore

A Metallica show at The Fillmore is an unforgettable experience, steeped in raw energy, intimacy, and legendary performances. The iconic San Francisco venue, known for its rich history and intimate atmosphere, creates the perfect backdrop for Metallica's high-octane performance. The band, typically known for playing massive arenas and stadiums, brings their unparalleled musicianship to a more personal, sweat-soaked setting where fans are just a few feet away from the stage. Expect to see Metallica ripping through classic tracks like "Enter Sandman," "Master of Puppets," and "One," while also mixing in some deeper cuts and surprises from their extensive catalog. The stage is often adorned with minimalistic yet powerful setups, allowing the focus to remain on the music. Fans, many of whom are die-hard, are in a frenzy, headbanging and moshing as the band delivers thunderous riffs and high-energy solos. The raw power and intensity of Metallica's performance at The Fillmore transforms the venue into a chaotic, electrifying celebration of metal, making it a truly one-of-a-kind show for both the band and the audience.
